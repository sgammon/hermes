appfactory Module¶

Platform: AppFactory

Deep integration with momentum labs’ Layer9 AppFactory platform. Exposes and injects shortcuts to code that controls, parses, and mediates signals between AppEngine and the individual AppFactory components: the Frontline, Upstream and Controller layers.
